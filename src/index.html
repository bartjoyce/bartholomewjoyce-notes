I am a web designer

Design Philosophy

My thinking has always been that good design is invisible: the aesthetics of a website should not distract the reader from its content. The less that stands between your audience and you the better. A website should be direct and clear in its delivery of information—showing the reader exactly what she wants to see and nothing more.

I communicate with my clients extensively, fleshing out the core purpose and content of their websites by meeting face-to-face. Once the purpose of the website is clear I attempt to find the most direct and effective way of conveying it.
